Deltoids
Biceps

Trapezius
01
Alternate Dumbbell Press
DUMBBELL
1
Sit on a bench with back support or on a seat.
2
Start with both dumbbells at shoulder level.
3
Press one dumbbell overhead until arm is straight.
4
Slowly lower and repeat with other arm.
5
Keep your abs engaged throughout the exercise.
Target: Anterior & Medial Deltoids
02
Bent-Over Lateral Raise
DUMBBELL
1
Stand feet shoulder-width apart; bend forward at hips.
2
Begin to bend your elbows slightly.
3
Palms facing each other, raise dumbbells to sides until parallel to floor.
4
Slowly lower to starting position and repeat.
Target: Rear (Posterior) Deltoids
03
Seated Military Press
BARBELL
1
Sit on bench with back support.
2
Grasp barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width.
3
Unlock elbows and press bar up over head.
4
Press until arms locked out overhead.
5
Return to just above shoulder level and repeat.
Target: All Three Deltoid Heads • Trapezius
04
Behind-The-Neck Press
BARBELL
1
Sit on bench with back straight.
2
Grasp barbell with a wide grip.
3
Lift and press bar behind head until arms locked out.
4
Return to just above shoulder level and repeat.
Target: Medial & Posterior Deltoids
05
Side Lateral Raise
DUMBBELL
1
Stand holding dumbbells at sides.
2
Keep slight bend in elbows; raise arms out to sides.
3
Raise until dumbbells are level with shoulders.
4
Pause, descend to start, repeat.
Target: Medial (Side) Deltoids
06
Barbell Front Raise
BARBELL
1
Stand feet shoulder-width apart; grasp barbell with overhand grip.
2
Elbows locked, raise barbell in front of body until shoulder height.
Target: Anterior (Front) Deltoids
07
Alternate Front Raise
DUMBBELL
1
Stand back straight; grasp dumbbell overhand at side.
2
Elbow locked, raise dumbbell to shoulder level.
3
Slowly lower and repeat with other arm.
Target: Anterior Deltoids
08
Low-Pulley Raise
CABLE
1
Stand shoulder next to low pulley; grasp handle with outside hand.
2
Arm straight, lift handle out to side until shoulder height.
3
Slowly lower and repeat.
Target: Medial Deltoids • Serratus Anterior

⚡ Shoulder Form & Alignment Showroom

🏋️

Overhead Pressing Arc

Drive weights vertically. Keep abs engaged, feet grounded, and neck neutral. Lock out at the top without shrugging.

Target: Anterior & Medial Deltoids
↔️

Lateral Raising Plane

Raise arms out to sides in a wide arc. Maintain a slight elbow bend. Lead with elbows to isolate the lateral head.

Target: Medial Deltoids
🔄

Rear Flye Extension

Hinge at hips, keep flat spine. Raise weights horizontally to squeeze rear deltoids. Control eccentric return.

Target: Posterior Deltoids

Triceps Workout

Triceps Brachii
Anconeus
01
Triceps Pushdown
CABLE
1
Stand elbows close to sides; grasp rope attachment.
2
Push the rope down until arms are fully extended.
3
Slowly return to starting position and repeat.
Target: All Three Triceps Heads
02
Seated Barbell Extension
BARBELL
1
Sit facing forward; grasp barbell with overhand grip.
2
Bending at elbows, lower barbell behind your head.
3
Slowly press back to starting position and repeat.
Target: Long Head of Triceps
03
Overhead Triceps Extension
DUMBBELL
1
Lie flat on bench holding dumbbell with both hands over chest.
2
Keeping elbows stationary, slowly lower dumbbell behind head.
3
Slowly extend arms back to starting position and repeat.
Target: Long Head of Triceps
04
One-Arm Dumbbell Extension
DUMBBELL
1
Hold one dumbbell overhead with arm fully extended.
2
Keep elbow in and stationary.
3
Bending at elbow, slowly lower dumbbell behind head.
Target: Long Head — Unilateral
05
Dumbbell Kickback
DUMBBELL
1
Place one knee on bench; lean forward holding dumbbell.
2
Grasp dumbbell with elbow bent and close to side.
3
Upper arm stationary, extend arm back until straight.
Target: Lateral & Medial Triceps Heads
06
Dumbbell Triceps Extension
DUMBBELL
1
Lie on back holding dumbbell above chest, arm fully extended.
2
Press dumbbell up by extending arm straight above chest.
3
Keep elbow stable to isolate the triceps.
Target: All Three Triceps Heads

⚡ Triceps Form & Alignment Showroom

👇

Cable Pushdown Extension

Keep elbows glued to ribs. Press rope down by flexing elbows only. Separate hands at bottom to fully activate outer head.

Target: Lateral & Medial Heads
☝️

Overhead Dumbbell Extension

Extend arms vertically. Keep upper arm completely locked and stationary. Lower weights deeply behind neck for a long-head stretch.

Target: Long Head of Triceps
↩️

Dumbbell Kickback Stroke

Align upper arm parallel to torso. Extend elbow backward to straight locked position. Avoid dropping elbow during swing.

Target: Lateral Head & Anconeus

Leg Curl

3
Slowly lower heels back and repeat.

3
Press weight back up; do not lock knees.

Leg Extension
MACHINE

Slowly lower weight and repeat.

Deep Barbell Squat

Place bar on traps, feet shoulder-width. Drive hips down and back to parallel. Keep knees pushed out, heels grounded.

Target: Hamstrings & Gluteals
👣

Seated Toe Raise Extension

Secure knees under pad. Push toes down to raise heels. Squeeze calves at top, lower deeply for full extension.

Target: Soleus & Gastrocnemius

01
Crunch
BODYWEIGHT
1
Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat, hands behind head.
2
Using abdominal strength, curl upper torso and shoulder blades off floor.
3
Do not pull up too high or strain your neck.
4
Return to starting position without resting and repeat.
Target: Rectus Abdominis
02
Decline Crunch
BENCH
1
Lie on back on decline bench; hook feet under roller pads.
2
Hands gently behind head (do not pull neck).
3
Curl upper torso and shoulder blades off bench.
4
Do not curl too high; return and repeat.
Target: Rectus Abdominis (increased range)
03
Raised Leg Crunch
BENCH
1
Lie on back, knees bent, calves across bench.
2
Arms and hands behind your head.
3
Curl upper torso and shoulder blades off floor and repeat.
Target: Upper Abdominals
04
Dumbbell Side Bend
DUMBBELL
1
Hold dumbbell in right hand with arm hanging straight down.
2
Slowly bend to the side, stretching opposite obliques.
3
Return to upright, repeat, then switch sides.
Target: Obliques — Internal & External
05
Crossover Crunch
BODYWEIGHT
1
Lie on back, knees bent, hands behind head.
2
Curl and rotate to one side, bringing opposite knee toward elbow.
3
Return to start, then switch sides.
Target: Obliques • Rectus Abdominis
06
Hanging Leg Raise
BAR
1
Hang from pull-up bar, hands shoulder-width, knees slightly bent.
2
Slowly pull knees or straight legs up toward chest using abs.
3
Slowly return to starting position and repeat. Avoid swinging.
Target: Lower Abdominals • Hip Flexors
07
Seated Twist
BARBELL
1
Sit on bench with wooden bar or light barbell on shoulders.
2
Slowly rotate upper torso to one side as far as comfortable.
3
Return to center and rotate to opposite side.
Target: Obliques • Serratus
08
Seated Knee Up
BENCH
1
Sit on edge of bench, hands gripping sides, lean slightly backward.
2
Extend both legs out with a slight bend in knees.
3
Slowly pull knees toward chest, contracting abs, and return.
Target: Lower Abdominals • Hip Flexors
